Autonomous car kit hardware

  • Autonomous Vehicle Platform:

    • Including a remote-controlled vehicle chassis, a basic remote controller, and a multi-mode joystick. It also consists of one battery (single cell with a minimum of 11.1V, 5200mAh), one servo motor, one motor, and one driver.

  • Electronic control system:

    • Consisting of a microcontroller and speed and steering drive modules.

    • Consisting of one 2D optical radar and a set of distance sensing modules (Ultrasound).
