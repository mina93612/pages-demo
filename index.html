About

The Vehicle Institute at Taipei Tech has been devoted to research and development in the field of autonomous vehicles. Our team constantly explores and applies the latest autonomous driving technologies to provide more intelligent, safe, and efficient transportation solutions.

Our research team combines expertise from various fields, including mechanical engineering, electronic engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence. We utilize advanced perception technologies, machine learning, and control algorithms to enable the perception, decision-making, and control capabilities of self-driving cars.

In addition to research projects, we collaborate with industry partners to undertake practical initiatives and translate our learning outcomes into real-world applications.

If you're interested in the field of autonomous vehicles, we welcome you to join our team. We look forward to exploring the future of autonomous driving technology together.
